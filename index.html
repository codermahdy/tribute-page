Humayun Ahmed

One of the most famous Bengali novelists of the 20th century

Here's a timeline of Humayun Ahmed:

  • 1948 - Born on 13 November 1948 in Kutubpur village under Mymensingh District, East Bengal, Dominion of Pakistan (now in Kendua Upazila, Netrokona District, Bangladesh)
  • 1965 - Passed the SSC examination from Bogra Zilla School in 1965
  • 1972 - Debut novel Nondito Noroke published in 1972
  • 1983 - The first television drama was "Prothom Prohor" (1983)
  • 1985 - The first drama serial was "Ei Shob Din Ratri" (1985)
  • 1988 - The historical drama series "Ayomoy" (1988)
  • 1990 - The urban drama series "Kothao Keu Nei" (1990)
  • 1994 - The first film was "Aguner Poroshmoni" (1994)
  • 2012 - Died on 19 July 2012 at Bellevue Hospital in New York City

"One golden age of Bengali literature ended with Tagore and Nazrul and another began with Humayun."

-- Bangladeshi Poet Al Mahmud
